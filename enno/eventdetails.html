Event Day Information

Registration is now closed. The schedule for E4K Day is as follows:

How do I know if I'm Team Blue or Team Gold?

At check-in, students will be divided into Team Blue and Team Gold.
Check-in will take place at Wheeler Hall from 9:30 am to 10:00 am.

Opening and Closing Ceremonies will be in 2050 Valley Life Sciences Building. Enter VLSB at the green arrow. All activities will be inside Wheeler Hall. Lunch will be provided in the courtyard of Dwinelle Hall.

Activity and Demo Descriptions

  • Marble Roller Coasters - learn the physics of roller coasters and build one out of foam
  • Electro-Magnetic Train - make a battery move using magnets
  • Square Wheels, Round Road - learn how a cart with square wheels can move
  • Garden with Industrial Engineers - form a manufacturing assembly line to plant seeds
  • A Smidge About Bridges - build a bridge and test its strength
  • Space Capsules - design a space capsule to protect an "egg-stronaut" from a fall
  • Gumdrop and Toothpick Towers - build a tower out of gumdrops and toothpicks
  • Penny Batteries - light up an LED using pennies
  • Popping Boba - learn about the chemical reactions behind popping boba
  • Twizzlers Half Life Challenge - discover radioactive decay using candy
  • Rolling Infrastructure - build a Rube Goldberg machine using everyday objects
  • Van de Graaff Generator--Shocking! (demo) - explore electricity with a high-voltage generator

All of these details and more will be on the pamphlet provided at check-in.
